Introduction

My name’s Vincent, I was born on May 19, 1997, in a tiny little red dot, called Singapore. Currently a second year student in Singapore Institute of Management – University of Wollongong, pursuing a degree, Bachelor of Business Information Systems (BBIS).

I am actually half-thai, BUT to be frank, I can barely make up full sentences in thai. As a matter of fact, one of my new year’s resolution is to be fluent and comfortable in speaking the language, so that I can communicate with my distant relatives.

Why BBIS?

When I was little, I’ve always been interested in making money from the things that I like to do.

For example, back in primary school days, Orbeez were in trend, they were these tiny little water beads that are meant to keep moisture in soil for plants, but in our tiny little innocent 8 year old brains, we thought they were little water babies and they could multiply if we grow them together in a bunch. For me, I see this as an opportunity to take advantage of the demand to make some money. So, I would buy a big bag of these Orbeez, which costs about $2, grow them myself, and sell it to my peers in small packages for $1 each. Till this day, I can still remember how much I made from this side hustle, $32. It may not seem much, but for an 8 year old, surviving on a $1.50 fishball noodle from the canteen for lunch, I felt like a millionaire.

Years have passed, technology have advanced, slowly as I grew up, I got interested in Information Technology. Hence, I thought to myself, why not both? And here I am, pursuing my degree in Business Information Systems.

Life before University

Before university and the army, I was studying in Temasek Polytechnic, Infocomm and Network Engineering (INE). Ever since I was exposed to their Freshmen Orientation Camp, I was hooked.

Being able to make so much friends, have numerous gatherings and outings, it was countless amount of fun. Being a part of this also helped me open up so much, helped me improve my communication skills, leading skills and also a better person to work with in group environments.
